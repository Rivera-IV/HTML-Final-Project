Our Mission at the Hound Hotel

Our goal at The Hound Hotel is to deliver a stellar service to families and their dogs by providing them with as much love and care as their families would.
We will ensure that your pup will have the utmost care with personalized treatment based on their individual needs. Ultimately, between our management and expertise, we can ensure that your pups will be happy and have healthy boarding with us at The Hound Hotel.

Our Reason for starting The Hound Hotel

Our reason for starting The Hound Hotel is to provide exceptional service to our customers and their pets. Additionally, we at The Hound Hotel offer daycare/boarding, grooming, and other supplies your dogs might need.
This Hound Hotel is about providing comfort and confidence to the owners that we have the ability to nurture and take care of their dog whether they are on vacation or having to work a busy work week. The Hound Hotel solution addresses key business issues by having a more convenient option for local places for locals and surrounding towns to drop off their pups and continue on with their errands.
